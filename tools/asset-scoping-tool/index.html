Asset Scoping Decision Tool

Walk through the classification decision for a single OT asset under NERC CIP, NIS2, or both. The tool produces a documented output — asset name, framework, classification, and the criteria that drove it — suitable for inclusion in your audit record.

How to use this tool

One asset at a time. Enter a name, select your framework, answer four questions, and print or copy the result for your audit record. Based on the NIS2 and NERC CIP Audit-Readiness Guide scoping methodology.
